## Review selection rule for voice sessions

1. Review overdue P0 topics first.
2. Ask 3–5 short closed-book questions.
3. Do not show notes until an answer is attempted.
4. Then continue with the next topic in `study-plan/progress-tracker.md`.
5. Update the status at the end of the session.

## Scoring rules

- Correct, structured and fluent: keep the next interval.
- Correct but hesitant: repeat in 2–3 days.
- Major technical error: return to D1.
- Cannot answer without notes: lower the mastery level.
- Do not grant Level 4 until two follow-ups and a trade-off are handled.
